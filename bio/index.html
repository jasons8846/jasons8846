I fathered a girl named Janelle on 5 November 2019 and we currently live in Belhar, Erica Township where we also attend the URC church.

My Schooling

I attended primary school at Cloetesville Primary from the years 1995 until 2001. I then attended Stellenbosch High School from the years 2002 and matriculated in 2006. I focused Maths, Economics, Computer Literacy and Accounting as my main subjects. After high school in 2007 I attended the Universtity of Stellenbosch and did my Bcomm Honours in Operations Research in 2010. I then also finished my Masters Degree in Operations Research in 2017 as a part time student.
